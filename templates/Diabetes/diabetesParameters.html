Parameters Related to Diabetes

  • Pregnancies:
    The number of times a woman has been pregnant.
  • Glucose:
    The level of glucose (sugar) in the blood.
  • Blood Pressure:
    The force of blood against the walls of the arteries.
  • Skin Thickness:
    The thickness of the skin at the triceps.
  • Insulin:
    The amount of insulin in the blood.
  • BMI:
    Body Mass Index, a measure of body fat based on height and weight.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function:
    A measure of the hereditary risk of developing diabetes.
  • Age:
    The age of the person in years.
